#unique_email(name, domain) ⇒ Object

Generate a unique email address that's compatible with gmail it works by appending '+' and a timestamp to the username portion of the email address. Gmail will ignore everything after the '+' and deliver the email.

This allows us to have one-time email addresses that all map back to the same common email account.

def unique_email(name, domain)
  "#{name}+#{Time.now.strftime '%Y%m%d%H%M'}@#{domain}"
end
